The tech.

Hand control

Direct drive

Wheel-mounted acceleration, braking, and emergency control for drivers with lower-limb impairments.

Head control

Sip and puff

Breath input controls throttle/brake while camera tracking converts head movement into steering.

Hybrid control

Sip and puff with direct drive

Manual steering with breath-controlled throttle and braking for hybrid adaptive control.
